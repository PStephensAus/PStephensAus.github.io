I am a Melbourne based data explorer searching for hidden insights within datasets. I have a background in both aviation and engineering, which provides me with unique skills and approaches to problems. In the aviation industry, I learnt the importance of procedure and precision. My engineering experience added on to this the knowledge and skills to take on advanced problems from computational fluid dynamics to research in biomechanics, using imported 3D scans to analyse geometry.

Data is ubiquitous, and I have decided to make it the focus of my future after experiencing a solution to a problem with a machine purely using data, in my previous industry. I am now looking to the future and building on what I have developed so far!
